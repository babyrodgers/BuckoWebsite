About Us

Listen to our debut single WINDOW!!!!!!

We're Ok Bucko, a band from Seattle, Washington. We like driving cars and listening to The Cars. Ok Bucko makes music that feels like you are talking to your best friend on the phone and folding laundry at the same time. And then you have to put the laundry down and switch to facetime to scream about something together.

What's Ok in Bucko world? We are about to release our FIRST EP this May! Our first single "Window" is already out. If you like what you see (and hear), follow us on the internet to stay in the Bucko loop.
