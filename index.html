About Us

Listen to our debut single WINDOW!!!!!!

Seattle based Ok Bucko cooks up sludgy power pop laced with the terror of a crumbling society, catchy hooks, and biting vocals. They serve all with a heavy side of TV references and absurdity.

Through Miranda Hardy’s crunchy guitar, Khyre Matthew’s ripping bass, and Zeke Bender’s dynamic drumming; Chelsea Rodgers’ keys and Hardy’s vocals add a sweet urgency to an already boiling pot.

Ok Bucko has been a long time in the making. The band arose out of the friendship of Hardy, Matthews, and Bender. The three cut their teeth together in DIY music, spending their late teens and early 20s booking shows, touring the country, and recording music on their own terms. Hardly Boys, Borscht Boys, Freddie Lee Toyoda and Titanic 2 are just some of the projects this friendship spans.

Adding Rodgers to the mix expands the sonic capabilities of the group. Renewing curiosity and inspiring melodic experimentation, Rodgers has been essential in finding the sound of Ok Bucko. Paired with the rock-solid rhythm section anchored by Matthews’ and Bender’s decades long friendship, the group finds its groove and stays there.
